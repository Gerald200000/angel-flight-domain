Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Angel's Flight ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Christian-themed mobile game developed for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Account (optional): If you sign in with Google, we collect your display name and Google user ID to save your score to the global leaderboard.
  • Game scores: Your best score is submitted to our online leaderboard when you play on Normal difficulty.
  • Device advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob to serve ads. You can reset or opt out of this in your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your name and score on the global leaderboard
  • To show advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To track your personal best score locally on your device

4. Advertising

Angel's Flight uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID and other data to show personalized ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting your device's Settings → Google → Ads.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob – for ad delivery (see section 4)
  • Google Firebase – for authentication
  • When required by law

6. Data Retention

Leaderboard scores are stored on our server as long as the service is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Angel's Flight is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The app does not require sign-in to play.

8. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your leaderboard entry
  • Play without signing in (no personal data collected)

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@angelsflight.app

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
