Who We Are

Angel Flight is a Christian-themed mobile game available on Android. The app is developed and maintained by SmartTools.

For any privacy-related inquiries, you can contact us at info@smarttools.co.za.

2

Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Google Sign-In (Optional)

Signing in is entirely optional. If you choose to sign in with your Google account, we collect:

  • Display name — shown on the global leaderboard
  • Google User ID — used to uniquely identify your leaderboard entry and prevent duplicate submissions
  • Profile photo URL — displayed in the welcome screen (not stored on our servers)

We do not collect your email address, phone number, or any other Google account data beyond what is listed above.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Game Data
  • Game scores — your best score on Normal difficulty is submitted to our online leaderboard when you play
  • Character selection — the character you use is saved locally on your device and submitted alongside your score
  • High score — stored locally on your device via SharedPreferences
  • Mission progress — stored locally on your device only; never sent to our servers
Device & Advertising Data (via Google AdMob)
  • Advertising ID (GAID) — used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements
  • IP address — collected by AdMob for geographic ad targeting and fraud prevention
  • Device type and OS version — used by AdMob to serve compatible ad formats
  • App interaction data — ad impressions and clicks tracked by AdMob

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical location or GPS data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Biometric data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Leaderboard functionality — to display your name and score on the global leaderboard so you can compete with other players
  • Preventing duplicate entries — your Google User ID ensures your leaderboard entry is updated rather than duplicated
  • Serving advertisements — to display banner ads via Google AdMob, which helps fund free access to the app
  • App improvement — aggregate, anonymised usage patterns may be used to improve gameplay balance and performance
  • Local personalisation — character selection and high scores are saved locally to preserve your progress between sessions
4

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable under data protection laws (including GDPR and POPIA), we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — you voluntarily choose to sign in with Google and have your score submitted to the leaderboard
  • Legitimate interests — operating the leaderboard and serving advertisements to maintain the free availability of the app
  • Legal obligation — where required by applicable law

You may withdraw consent at any time by signing out of the app or contacting us to remove your data.

5

Advertising & Google AdMob

Angel Flight displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob is an advertising platform operated by Google LLC.

What AdMob Collects

  • Device Advertising ID (Android GAID)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Device model, OS version, language settings
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

Personalised vs Non-Personalised Ads

By default, AdMob may serve personalised ads based on your interests and browsing history. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time:

  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • Or reset your Advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID

AdMob Privacy Policy

For full details on how Google processes data for advertising purposes, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Public leaderboard — your display name and score are visible to all players on the global leaderboard. You can avoid this by playing without signing in.
  • Google AdMob — device advertising data is shared with Google for ad delivery as described in Section 5.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business transfer — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the new owner, who will be bound by this Privacy Policy.
8

Data Retention

  • Leaderboard data — your name and score are retained on our servers for as long as the leaderboard service is active, or until you request deletion.
  • Local device data — high scores, character selections, and mission progress are stored on your device indefinitely until the app is uninstalled or you clear app data.
  • Advertising data — governed by Google AdMob's own retention policies.

To request deletion of your leaderboard data, contact us at info@smarttools.co.za with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include your Google display name.

9

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption — all data transmitted between the app and our server is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS
  • Secure hosting — our leaderboard server is hosted on Railway with MongoDB Atlas, both of which employ industry-standard security practices
  • Minimal data collection — we only collect what is strictly necessary to operate the leaderboard
  • No passwords stored — authentication is handled entirely by Google; we never see or store your Google password

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Angel Flight is a family-friendly Christian game suitable for all ages. We take children's privacy seriously.

  • The app does not require sign-in to play — children can play anonymously without any personal data being collected
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13
  • If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at info@smarttools.co.za and we will promptly delete it
  • Advertisements are served via Google AdMob. We recommend parents review Google's Family Link tools for additional controls

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

General Rights

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to object — object to certain types of processing
  • Right to portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing

South African Users (POPIA)

If you are located in South Africa, you are protected under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Submit a complaint to the Information Regulator of South Africa at inforegulator.org.za

EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@smarttools.co.za. We will respond within 30 days.

International Data Transfers

Our leaderboard server is hosted via Railway and MongoDB Atlas, which may process data in data centres outside of South Africa. By using Angel Flight and submitting a score to the leaderboard, you consent to this transfer.

We ensure that any such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards, and our hosting providers comply with applicable data protection standards.
