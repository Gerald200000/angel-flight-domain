How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Angel Flight account and associated data, follow these steps:

1

Send an email to info@smarttools.co.za with the subject line:
"Angel Flight – Delete My Account"

2

Include your Google display name (the name shown on the leaderboard) so we can identify your account.

3

We will process your request within 7 days and confirm via email once your data has been deleted.

Email Us to Delete Account

What gets deleted & what is kept

  • Deleted: Your display name and leaderboard score from our servers
  • Deleted: Your Google User ID from our database
  • Kept temporarily: Server access logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security purposes, after which they are automatically purged
  • Not held by us: Local data (high score, character selection) stored on your device — uninstall the app to remove it
  • Not held by us: Google account data and AdMob advertising data — managed by Google directly
